What is Barcade ?


Bar

Barcade has an award winning bar, beergarden and nightclub which spans three floors. There is an extensive selection of locally sourced craft beers, cocktails and bar bites available. You can enjoy your selection in the lounge bar, the heated outdoor terrace or the nightclub.

Arcade

With over 50 oldschool arcade machines to pick from, Barcade will never fail to provide that rush of nostalgia and euphoria during every visit. If you want to protect the galaxy from space invaders, save Peach from Bowser or leapfrog across a busy road to victory, we have a machine for you !
